5 years Superior Gold Rum

Amori Di Angostura(70cl)

5yrs Superior Gold Rum

Angostura® 5 YR old rum is an exquisite blend of light gold rums, aged for a minimum of five years in used bourbon oak barrels and distilled in Angostura’s multi-column continuous still in Trinidad. Carefully filtered through charcoal before blending, this is a luminescent clear rum with subtle golden highlights. An exquisite blend of light rums, this rum is aromatic with light woody notes of coconut, caramel, lemon, hints of vanilla and nuances of tropical fruits.

TASTING NOTES

  1. Colour: a golden luminescent colour with green and orange reflections on the edge of the glass
  2. Nose: a strong, aromatic nose with light woody notes of coconut, caramel, lemon zest and a hint of vanilla
  3. Taste: soft and delicate with nuances of tropical fruits, mangoes and bananas
  4. Finish: quick and lightly spiced with a sweet buttery fade

ANGOSTURA® 5 YEAR OLD RUM CHARACTERISTICS

  • Easy to mix and incredibly versatile
  • Great for tropical based cocktails
  • Winner of Double Gold (Spirit)

Amaro Di Angostura *70CL

Description

Amaro Di Angostura : As the worldwide leader in aromatic bitters, the brand’s rich heritage provided the expertise and integrity to produce a new type of Amaro that is truly one-of-a-kind. The House of Angostura® blenders combined its iconic ANGOSTURA® aromatic bitters with neutral spirit and added more spices until a magnificent herbal liqueur was created. The spirit, spices and bitter herbs were mixed and then left to marry for three months. The result, Amaro di ANGOSTURA®, is an elevation of a classic liqueur creating an entirely new world dimension to the category.

Bottled at 35% ABV, Amaro di ANGOSTURA® is a deep amber colour, with aromas of cinnamon, dark chocolate and the unmistakable ANGOSTURA® aromatic bitters. The flavours explode on the tongue with warm cinnamon and liquorice notes. Amaro di ANGOSTURA® is a lush liqueur that provides bartenders a new world
